



                                                                      ONE STOP CBSE 
                                                                 A Great Place To Learn

   
           One Stop Cbse is a easy to use app made for students stuyding in grade 8 , 9 and 10 . As the name suggests it follows the CBSE curriculum . 
           The app contains three screens . The first screen has the all the grades in it . In the second screen you can select the subject you
           want to study . The third screen lets you choose the chapter .
